📝 How to Apply Civil Court Ranchi Offline Form 2026
1.Download the form from official website https://ranchi.dcourts.gov.in or use format given in notification on A-4 size paper
2.Fill the form in your own handwriting in English using CAPITAL LETTERS
3.Affix 3 Passport size photos: 1 on form, 1 on admit card, 1 extra
4.Attach self-attested copies of 10th Marksheet and Caste Certificate
5.Attach self-addressed 25cm x 11cm envelope with Rs. 40/- postage stamp
6.Send form via Registered/Speed Post to:
Prabhari Nyayadheesh, Vyavhar Nyayalaya, Ranchi
7.Write on envelope: "Advertisement No-01/2026, Application for Post Peon/Orderly, Category ___"
