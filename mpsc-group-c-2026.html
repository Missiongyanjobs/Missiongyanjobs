❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is the last date to apply for MPSC Group C Recruitment 2026?

Ans: The online application window opens on June 27, 2026, and the final date to submit your online application form is July 19, 2026.

Q2. What is the educational qualification required for Clerk-Typist post?

Ans: Candidates must possess a Bachelor's Degree (Graduation) in any discipline from a recognized university. Additionally, a government-recognized typing certificate with speed of 30 WPM in Marathi OR 40 WPM in English is mandatory.

Q3. Is there an interview stage in MPSC Group C selection process?

Ans: No, there is no interview for any posts under MPSC Group C. Final selection is strictly based on marks obtained in Main Examination, followed by qualifying Skill Test/Physical Test (where applicable).

Q4. Can final year graduation students apply for MPSC Group C 2026?

Ans: No, candidates must have already completed graduation and possess final degree/marksheet at the time of filling online application form.

Q5. What is the negative marking criteria for MPSC Group C Exam?

Ans: Yes, there is negative marking of 1/4th (0.25 marks) for every incorrect answer. This rule applies to both Preliminary and Main Examination.
